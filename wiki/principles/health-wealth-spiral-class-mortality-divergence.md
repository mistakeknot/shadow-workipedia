# Health Wealth Spiral Class Mortality Divergence

Top quintile 15 years longer life expectancy than bottom + 95%+ coverage top vs 80% bottom + 2× preventive care utilization → illness → lost income → job loss → disability trap → bankruptcy drains savings/credit/assets → generational transfer (medical debt functionally inherited) → poor→sicker→poorer→sicker feedback acceleration (health inequality drives wealth inequality, wealth inequality drives health inequality)
