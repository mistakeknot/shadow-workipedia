# Contagion Adoption S Curve

Discrete ideologies and social movements spread via logistic diffusion curves with vector-trait feedback loops, where incompatible traits suppress each other, creating winner-take-most cultural dominance and rapid state transitions once adoption exceeds 15-20% threshold.
