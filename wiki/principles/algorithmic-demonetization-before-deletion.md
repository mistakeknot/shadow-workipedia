# Algorithmic Demonetization Before Deletion

Platforms suppress reach and revenue through algorithmic demonetization ("shadow banning") before explicit bans, creating economic death without the optics of censorship. YouTube's 2017+ "adpocalypse" shifted creators from millions in revenue to near-zero while technically remaining "on platform."
