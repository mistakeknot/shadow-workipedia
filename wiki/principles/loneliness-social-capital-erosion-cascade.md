# Loneliness Social Capital Erosion Cascade

Serious loneliness prevalence rises 20%→36% post-2020 as average close friends decline 3.0→1.5 (1985→2021), civic organization membership drops 40%→<20%, and religious attendance falls 50%→<30%, with >40% loneliness threshold triggering community cohesion breakdown and political extremism vulnerability (isolated individuals more radicalized).
