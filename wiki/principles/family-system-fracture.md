# Family System Fracture

Dementia care falls disproportionately on daughters (65-70% of primary caregivers female) → career disruption → retirement insecurity → sibling conflict over care responsibility → divorce rates elevated 6x → family relationships destroyed alongside patient (dementia consumes families)
