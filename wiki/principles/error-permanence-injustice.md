# Error Permanence Injustice

Credit report errors affect 25-40% of reports (FTC study), yet dispute processes favor agencies. Errors from identity theft, medical billing, or data entry persist for years. The burden of proof falls on consumers who lack resources or expertise to challenge algorithmic judgments.
