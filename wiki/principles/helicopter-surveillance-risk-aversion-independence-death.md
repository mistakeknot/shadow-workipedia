# Helicopter Surveillance Risk Aversion Independence Death

Parents arrested for kids walking to park → "stranger danger" despite crime decline → constant supervision required → risk-taking eliminated → outdoor play requires adult presence → children never unsupervised until 18 → independence development impossible → anxiety generation created.
