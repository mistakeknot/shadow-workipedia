# Wage Differential Pull Factor Dominance

A nurse earning $2,000/month in Philippines can earn $6,000/month in Saudi Arabia and $8,000/month in US. These 3-4x differentials overwhelm local retention incentives. Global labor markets for healthcare workers create permanent southward brain drain that no domestic policy can counteract without addressing wage fundamentals.
