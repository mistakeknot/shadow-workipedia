# Wage Differential Magnets

Healthcare workers flow from low-wage to high-wage countries following 5-10× salary differentials. A Filipino nurse earning $5,000/year can earn $60,000 in the US—the economic pull is irresistible regardless of ethical concerns or source country needs.
