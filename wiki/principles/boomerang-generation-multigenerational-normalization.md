# Boomerang Generation Multigenerational Normalization

52% of young adults living with parents (2020, highest since Great Depression) → "failure to launch" reframed as "multigenerational living" → parental burden vs extended support → household economics shift → privacy and autonomy sacrifice → adult development arrested.
