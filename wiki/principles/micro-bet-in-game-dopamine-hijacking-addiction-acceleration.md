# Micro Bet In Game Dopamine Hijacking Addiction Acceleration

Pre-legalization: bet before game, wait hours/days → post-legalization: bet every play, every minute → "next touchdown scorer" "next three-pointer" → dopamine hit every 30 seconds → variable reward schedule identical to slot machines → in-game betting 50%+ of handle → 10× more addictive than pre-game → betting apps engineered by slot machine designers (IGT, Scientific Games) → young male demographic (18-35) most vulnerable → sports viewing transformed into gambling session.
