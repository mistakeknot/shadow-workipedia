# Accountability Diffusion No One Responsible

When algorithm produces wrongful eviction or wage garnishment, liability fragments: vendor claims algorithm is tool, court claims discretion, plaintiff claims reliance on system. No party bears full responsibility. Appeals processes assume human error, not systematic algorithmic bias.
