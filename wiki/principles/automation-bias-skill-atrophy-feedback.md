# Automation Bias Skill Atrophy Feedback

Once clinicians routinely defer to AI recommendations, diagnostic reasoning skills atrophy within 3-5 years. Physicians who override AI face liability exposure when wrong, but AI failures trigger "automation bias" where clinicians accept obviously incorrect recommendations because "the computer said so."
