# Shelter Capacity Barrier Cascade Exclusion

Barrier penalties (sobriety 25% + ID 20% + curfew 15% + pets 10% + religion 10%) create cumulative cap 60% exclusion rate, reducing effective capacity = nominal beds × (1 - barriers) × (1 - quality penalties), where LA 20% nominal capacity becomes 8% effective capacity (20% × 0.40 remaining), forcing 75% turnaway rate and accelerating unsheltered encampment formation
