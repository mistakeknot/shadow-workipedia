# COVID Acceleration Touch Aversion Normalization Persistence

2020-2022 physical distancing → 60% reduction in non-household touch → post-pandemic "touch anxiety" persisting in 30-40% of population → handshake hesitancy, hug avoidance normalized → professional touch (massage, physical therapy) stigmatized → hygiene theater permanently elevating touch fear baseline.
