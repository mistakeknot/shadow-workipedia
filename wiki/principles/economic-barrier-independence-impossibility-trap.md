# Economic Barrier Independence Impossibility Trap

Median home price 7x income (1970: 2.5x) → median rent 40%+ of income → student debt $35k average → entry wages stagnant 40 years → independence economically impossible → living with parents required not chosen → adulthood gated by wealth transfer.
