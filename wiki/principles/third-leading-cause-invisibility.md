# Third Leading Cause Invisibility

Medical errors are the 3rd leading cause of death in the US (250,000-440,000 annually) but are systematically undercounted because death certificates list the underlying disease, not the error. This statistical invisibility prevents the crisis from generating political pressure for reform.
