# Gig Economy Fragmentation Quality Erosion

Telehealth platforms, travel nursing agencies, and locum services extract clinicians from stable institutional employment into episodic gig work. Gig workers earn more but institutions lose continuity, mentorship capacity, and quality. Gig fragmentation accelerates during crises (COVID travel nurse wages $10K/week) then becomes structural.
