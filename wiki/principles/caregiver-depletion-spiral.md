# Caregiver Depletion Spiral

Each dementia patient requires 1.5-3 caregivers over disease course (burnout, death, divorce rotate primary caregivers) → 11M caregivers now → 16M+ needed by 2050 → working-age population growing slower than need (caregiver supply mathematically insufficient)
