# Dependency Trap Fragility

As societies mandate digital ID for banking, benefits, healthcare, and travel, they create single points of failure. When India's Aadhaar authentication fails, 500M people lose access to rations. Digital ID dependency creates systemic vulnerability to outages, breaches, and targeted exclusion.
