# Cultural Political Transplant Conflict

California refugees to Texas bring voting patterns that shift purple states blue; Austin went from +11 Biden to +25 Biden equivalent by 2024; locals perceive "colonization" while newcomers expect destination to adapt to them (migration creates political instability)
