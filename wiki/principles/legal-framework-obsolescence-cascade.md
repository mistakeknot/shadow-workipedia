# Legal Framework Obsolescence Cascade

Family law, estate law, pension systems, and social security all assume ~80-year lifespans; longevity breakthroughs invalidate actuarial tables, marriage contracts, and inheritance expectations simultaneously, creating system-wide legal chaos faster than legislative adaptation.
