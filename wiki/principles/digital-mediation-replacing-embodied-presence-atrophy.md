# Digital Mediation Replacing Embodied Presence Atrophy

50% of social interaction now screen-mediated → touch-based communication declining 3-5%/year → younger generations normalize touchless relationships → "virtual hug" substitutes provide 0% oxytocin benefit → embodied social skills atrophying → physical presence feels awkward/threatening → further retreat to digital.
