# Regime Transition Multi Pathway

Legitimacy, revolutionary, and authoritarian forces accumulating independently, with elections activating <60 days if democratic institutions >40% trust, coups triggered if military loyalty <40%, and revolutions at force >70 and legitimacy <20 simultaneously
