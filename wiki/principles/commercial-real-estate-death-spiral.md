# Commercial Real Estate Death Spiral

Office vacancy above 20% triggers landlord defaults → bank losses → reduced lending → more defaults; downtown ecosystems (restaurants, retail, transit) collapse simultaneously creating unrecoverable urban cores (vacancy begets vacancy)
