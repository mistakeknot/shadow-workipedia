# Diffusion Gradient Espionage

Knowledge diffuses via trade partners and treaties with 1-2 year lag for treaty participants versus 5-10 years via network diffusion, while espionage exposure risk creates diplomatic tension, enabling technology transfer through collaboration (legitimate), trade (organic), or theft (contested).
