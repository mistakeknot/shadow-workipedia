# Housing First Success Contagion Adoption

Housing First retention ≥85% (Utah/Houston proven) → +0.20 legitimacy boost + +5-10% allocation increase + demonstration effect spreads to 30% of observing neighbors within 2-3 years, creating virtuous feedback where success breeds adoption while cost savings ($35k jail vs $15k housing) become politically salient to fiscal conservatives, breaking partisan gridlock
