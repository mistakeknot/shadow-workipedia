# Leverage Frequency Cascade

Leverage >25× changes crisis frequency 1/30→1/10 years and enables 5-round margin call cascades (-40-60% cumulative decline when correlation >0.75), creating permanent instability where bailout fatigue accumulates faster than crises can be prevented
