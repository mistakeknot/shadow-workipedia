# Due Process Formalism Vs Reality

Constitutional protections assume meaningful opportunity to be heard. When algorithms process cases in seconds, hearings become rubber stamps. Defendants receive notice but not comprehension; appearance opportunity exists but time off work costs more than judgment. Form satisfies law while substance violates it.
