# Parallel China Dynamics

The US credit system exhibits structural similarities to China's Social Credit System—algorithmic scoring, opaque criteria, life-domain consequences, difficulty of redemption—differing mainly in private vs. state administration. Both create stratified societies with excluded underclasses.
