# Bullwhip Demand Distortion

Small demand changes at retail amplify exponentially upstream—10% consumer surge becomes 30% distributor order becomes 100% manufacturer order becomes 300% component order—creating phantom shortages, inventory hoarding, and price spikes that persist months after original disruption ends.
