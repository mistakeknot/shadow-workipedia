# Data Broker Surveillance

Credit bureaus are surveillance intermediaries aggregating purchase data, address history, employment records, and social connections. The "alternative data" expansion (utility payments, rent, phone bills, social media) creates comprehensive behavioral profiles exceeding official credit history.
