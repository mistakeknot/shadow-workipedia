# Defund Reversal Backlash Ratchet

Property damage >$500M + crime surge >20% (real or perceived) → Law & Order satisfaction <0.30 → 70% defund reversals within 24 months (2021-2023 pattern), locking in ratchet effect where reform gets attempted then dismantled, demoralizing movement for next 12-18 month cycle
