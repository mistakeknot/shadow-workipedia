# Peter Pan Syndrome Permanent Youth Identity Crisis

Extended adolescence → "adulting" as foreign concept → responsibilities avoided → commitments deferred → identity based on consumption not production → perpetual youth culture → "30 is the new 20" normalizes delay → maturity markers abandoned.
