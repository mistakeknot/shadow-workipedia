# HHI Lobbying Influence Multiplier

Market concentration (HHI 2500-10000) gates lobbying power multipliers of 2.5-5×, compounded by alliance network effectiveness (15-40% per ally), enabling corporate regulatory capture and transnational power projection that overwhelms individual governments—historical example: United Fruit Company Central American intervention.
