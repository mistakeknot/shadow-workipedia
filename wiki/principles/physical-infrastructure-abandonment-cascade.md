# Physical Infrastructure Abandonment Cascade

As population shifts to virtual existence, maintenance of physical infrastructure (roads, buildings, utilities) becomes economically unsupportable; declining physical world quality accelerates virtual migration, creating self-reinforcing abandonment spiral.
