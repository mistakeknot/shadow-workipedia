# Demilitarization Cultural Lock

Institutional pacifist cultural shifts (like post-WWII Japan) of 0.10-0.30 magnitude create self-reinforcing demilitarization where cultural pacifism suppresses military institutional growth (budget starvation) and recruitment fails, making remilitarization impossible without external shock and cultural reversal.
