# Cost Pressure Quality Tradeoff Inevitability

Hospitals facing 1-3% operating margins adopt AI primarily to cut labor costs, not improve care. AI triage reduces staffing 30-50%; AI documentation eliminates scribes; AI diagnostics enable non-specialist oversight. Cost savings materialize immediately; quality problems emerge over years through mortality statistics.
