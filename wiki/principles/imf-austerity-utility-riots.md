# IMF Austerity Utility Riots

When debt service becomes unsustainable, IMF programs demand utility price hikes (water, electricity, transit fares) and social spending cuts that trigger protests/riots—Ecuador 2019 fuel subsidy riots, Sudan 2018 bread riots—destabilizing governments while foreign creditors extract repayment.
