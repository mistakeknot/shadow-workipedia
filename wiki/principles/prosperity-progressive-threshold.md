# Prosperity Progressive Threshold

GDP >$10,000 per capita triggers Inglehart post-materialist drift toward progressive values, compounded by +0.001/year urbanization bonus, creating self-reinforcing affluence-driven cultural transformation that reverses only through economic collapse.
