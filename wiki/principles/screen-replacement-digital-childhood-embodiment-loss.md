# Screen Replacement Digital Childhood Embodiment Loss

Average child 7+ hours screen time/day → screens replace physical play → video games replace neighborhood games → social media replaces face-to-face → embodied play skills atrophying → virtual childhood = developmental mismatch → 10,000 years of play evolution abandoned.
