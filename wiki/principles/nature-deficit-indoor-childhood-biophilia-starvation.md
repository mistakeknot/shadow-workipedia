# Nature Deficit Indoor Childhood Biophilia Starvation

Children spend 90%+ of time indoors → "nature deficit disorder" (Louv) → average child can't identify common birds/trees → outdoor play rare → green space access declining → biophilia needs unmet → attention restoration blocked → ADHD symptoms from environment.
