# Serial Monogamy Normalization Resistance

Century-long lifespans would logically support 4-5 sequential marriages of 20-25 years each; religious and traditional institutions resist this normalization, creating sustained culture wars over marriage definition as fundamental as previous LGBTQ+ marriage debates.
