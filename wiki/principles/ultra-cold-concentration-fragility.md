# Ultra Cold Concentration Fragility

Advanced vaccines (mRNA, viral vectors) require ultra-cold storage (-70°C to -20°C); this capability concentrates in wealthy nations and urban centers, creating geographic fragility—any disruption leaves rural/poor populations without viable vaccines.
