# Rerouting Cost Cascade

Alternative routes impose massive penalties: Cape of Good Hope adds 10-14 days and $1M+ fuel per voyage around Africa; Arctic routes require ice-class vessels ($20-50M premium) and seasonal windows; rerouting costs compound as all traffic shifts simultaneously, creating secondary congestion.
