# Viral Incident Amplification Feedback

Video evidence + unarmed victim + >10M views triggers national movement formation (Ferguson 2014: 10M views → 140k protesters, George Floyd 2020: 350M views → 20M participants), with each 10× increase in virality creating 0.30 movement strength multiplier and collapsing response time from 2 weeks to 48 hours
