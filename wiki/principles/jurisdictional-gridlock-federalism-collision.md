# Jurisdictional Gridlock Federalism Collision

Municipal ban count accumulates when `stateRiskPoolCount ≥ 5 AND uninsuredFleetPercent > 15%` → federal preemption triggers at `municipalBanCount ≥ 10` (interstate commerce clause vs police powers) → court challenges active ≥5 simultaneously → regulatory fragmentation 0→1.0 as cities impose different rules (geofencing, speed limits, curfews) → prisoners' dilemma: cities want to ban for safety but fear losing tech jobs → balkanized deployment when court battles unresolved >5 years.
