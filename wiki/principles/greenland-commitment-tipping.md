# Greenland Commitment Tipping

+0.1°C warming → +7% melt rate acceleration that commits 2-3m sea level rise at +2.0-2.5°C, creating path dependency where coastal infrastructure investments become stranded assets once surface elevation feedback activates (centennial reversal timescale).
