# Corporate Consolidation Seed Sovereignty Loss

4 companies 60% global seeds (Bayer, Corteva, Syngenta, BASF) + 85% US beef processing + 70% grain trading (ABCD oligopoly) → vertical integration lock-in → farmer contract dependency → patent enforcement seed saving prohibition → 43.4M acres US farmland foreign-owned → expropriation triggers if critical threshold reached (concentration enables control from seed to processing, nations vulnerable)
