# Cultural Distance Alliance Gate

Alliance formation requires cultural distance <0.30, creating ideological clustering that locks institutions into compatibility networks and prevents cross-ideological power consolidation, enabling Cold War-style institutional bifurcation where universities, militaries, and churches sort into incompatible rival networks.
