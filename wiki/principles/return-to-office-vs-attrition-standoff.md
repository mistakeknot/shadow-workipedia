# Return To Office Vs Attrition Standoff

Mandating return loses 10-30% of workforce (disproportionately top performers with options); allowing full remote loses 15-25% of collaboration/innovation benefit; hybrid compromise satisfies no one fully (no equilibrium solution exists)
