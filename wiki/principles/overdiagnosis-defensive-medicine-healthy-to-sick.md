# Overdiagnosis Defensive Medicine Healthy To Sick

Excessive screening finds non-threatening conditions → incidentaloma cascades (one finding → more tests → more findings) → defensive medicine from lawsuit fear → financial incentives for procedures → patient anxiety → unnecessary treatment causes real harm (converting healthy people into patients, fear drives overtesting, profit from finding "disease")
