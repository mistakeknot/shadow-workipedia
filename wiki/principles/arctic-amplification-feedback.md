# Arctic Amplification Feedback

2-4× warming rate multiplier (Arctic +0.75°C/decade vs +0.19°C globally) creating irreversible ice-albedo cascade where summer Arctic ice-free conditions lock in century-scale heat persistence above +1.5°C.
