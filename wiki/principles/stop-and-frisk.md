# Stop And Frisk

NYC program (2002-2013), 685k stops at peak (2011), 88% innocent, 52% Black 31% Hispanic (NYCLU)
- **School-to-Prison Pipeline**: Zero-tolerance policies (1990s-2000s), school police → juvenile arrests → adult incarceration pipeline
- **Three Strikes Laws**: California (1994-2012) disproportionately impacts Black defendants, 8,500 life sentences (45% for nonviolent offenses)
- **Mandatory Minimums**: Federal drug laws (1986, 1988) remove judicial discretion, average sentence 5-10 years regardless of circumstances
