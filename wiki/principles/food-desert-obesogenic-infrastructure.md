# Food Desert Obesogenic Infrastructure

Food deserts lack healthy options → food swamps excess fast food → supermarket redlining poor areas → car-dependent sprawl prevents activity → walkability zero → environmental justice concentration → built environment drives obesity (obesogenic environment structurally determined, poor trapped in car-dependent food deserts, wealthy access walkable neighborhoods + quality food)
