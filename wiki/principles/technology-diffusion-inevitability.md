# Technology Diffusion Inevitability

Nuclear weapons knowledge from 1945 cannot be uninvented—every decade more states acquire technical capability, with proliferation cascades (Israel→Iran→Saudi, India→Pakistan) demonstrating that regional dynamics override global nonproliferation norms.
