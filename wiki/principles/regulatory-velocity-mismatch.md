# Regulatory Velocity Mismatch

FDA device approval averages 18-24 months; AI models update daily. By the time a medical AI receives regulatory clearance, it may have been retrained dozens of times. The cleared version no longer exists. Software-as-Medical-Device (SaMD) frameworks lag a decade behind deployment reality.
