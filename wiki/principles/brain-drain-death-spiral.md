# Brain Drain Death Spiral

When a country loses 30%+ of its physicians/nurses, remaining staff face unsustainable workloads → more burnout → more emigration → worse ratios → accelerated exodus. Jamaica, Philippines, and several African countries have crossed this threshold with near-total collapse of domestic healthcare.
