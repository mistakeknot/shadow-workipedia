# Anti Immortality Backlash Intensity

Those unable to afford longevity treatments develop intense resentment toward immortal elite; "lifespan cap" movements gain political power from mortality-bound majority, potentially leading to regeneration clinic attacks, wealth seizure, or forced mortality legislation.
