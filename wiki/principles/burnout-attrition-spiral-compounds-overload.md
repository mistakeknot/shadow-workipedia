# Burnout Attrition Spiral Compounds Overload

Low salaries ($50k-80k vs $150k+ private), impossible caseloads, and moral injury drive 30-50% annual turnover, meaning the defenders who remain become even more overloaded while inexperienced replacements handle complex cases.
