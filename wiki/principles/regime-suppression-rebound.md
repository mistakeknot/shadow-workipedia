# Regime Suppression Rebound

Authoritarian regimes suppress trait expression but cannot prevent underground diffusion during leadership transitions, creating cultural backlash where suppressed traits explode in adoption post-liberation—historical example: Soviet collapse → immediate Westernization surge.
