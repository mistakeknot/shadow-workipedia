# Civil Conflict

## Overview

The Civil Conflict system models internal armed conflicts, insurgencies, civil wars, and the conditions that generate or resolve them.

## What It Simulates

- **Rebel group formation and capacity**
- **Government counterinsurgency**
- **Conflict duration and intensity**
- **Peace processes and settlements**

## How It Affects Gameplay

- Civil wars devastate development
- Conflict traps are hard to escape
- External intervention shapes outcomes
