# Military

## Overview

The Military system models armed forces capabilities, defense spending, military technology, and the use of force in international relations.

## What It Simulates

- **Force structure and capabilities**
- **Defense spending and procurement**
- **Military technology advancement**
- **Power projection and deterrence**

## How It Affects Gameplay

- Military strength enables or constrains foreign policy
- Arms races consume resources
- War as failure of other systems
