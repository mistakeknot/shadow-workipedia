# Education

## Overview

The Education system models human capital development, literacy, workforce skills, and the long-term effects of educational investment.

## What It Simulates

- **Primary/secondary enrollment and quality**
- **Higher education and research**
- **Vocational training and skills**
- **Educational inequality**

## How It Affects Gameplay

- Education investment has 15-20 year lag
- Workforce quality affects economic potential
- Brain drain and retention challenges
