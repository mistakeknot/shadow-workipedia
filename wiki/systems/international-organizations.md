# International Organizations

## Overview

The International Organizations system models multilateral institutions—UN, IMF, regional bodies—and their capacity to coordinate global action.

## What It Simulates

- **UN and specialized agencies**
- **Regional organizations (EU, AU, ASEAN)**
- **Financial institutions (IMF, World Bank)**
- **Treaty regimes and compliance**

## How It Affects Gameplay

- IOs provide coordination mechanisms
- Legitimacy affects compliance
- Institutional reform is slow
